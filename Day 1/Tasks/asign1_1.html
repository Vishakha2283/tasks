KBP COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING SATARA

ABOUT COLLEGE :-

Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil College of Engineering and Polytechnic, Satara was established in 1983 under Rayat Shikshan Sanstha in Maharashtra.College offers a huge, beautiful and serene campus with residential facilities to the students with separate hostels for girls and boys. Mission of the college is “To produce excellent technocrats through academic excellence” There are modern and upgraded lab facilities to help the students perform their research work and experiments Nearest railway station is Satara that is 8km from the college and nearest airport is pune that is 130 km from the college College is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi and affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur
